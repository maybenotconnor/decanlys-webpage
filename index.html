The Playbook

From AI-unsure to AI-native.

Many organizations don't have an AI problem — they have an awareness problem. I guide your team through a proven framework that respects their expertise while embedding AI into how they work. No hype. No mandates. Just a clear path from skepticism to competence.

AI-Unsure

1

Understand

Before introducing any tools, I learn how your business actually works: where time gets wasted, what frustrates people, and where the real opportunities are. I map workflows, not feature lists.

2

Introduce

The first move is an invitation Your team tries AI on one low-risk task. I listen to what they're actually concerned about and build from there.

3

Activate

Peer champions, people your team already respects, lead the way. Real use cases address real frustrations. A proof-of-concept team shows results everyone can see.

4

Scale

From individual adoption to organizational standard. Shared knowledge bases, approved workflows, and training materials built by your own people. Measured by outcomes, not usage numbers.

5

Sustain

AI becomes how your organization works, not a side tool. Integrated into onboarding, embedded in operations, continuously evolving. When the champion program isn't needed anymore — you're done.

AI-Native

What gets built along the way

Workflow Automation

I find the manual, repetitive tasks eating your team's time, like data entry, document handling, and report generation — and build AI-powered systems that handle them.

Document Intelligence

Paper forms, PDFs, scattered files — I make them searchable and useful. Your team finds what they need in seconds, and AI extracts the data that matters.

Custom AI Tools

Chatbots, internal dashboards, smart assistants — whatever your business needs, I design and build it. Nothing more than you need.

Every engagement is tailored to your organization's size, culture, and pace — from a 4-week sprint with a small team to a 6-month transformation. Have a different challenge? I'd love to hear about it →
